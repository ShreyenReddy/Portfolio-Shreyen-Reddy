Biography

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Shreyen Reddy. I am an aspiring Game Designer and Software Developer who loves combining creativity with technical problem-solving to build interactive technologies and experiences. What draws me to software and design is that anything is possible; the only limit is my imagination, and I am driven by the challenge of turning bold ideas into polished, engaging experiences.

I am currently a final-year Bachelor of Engineering Science (Digital Arts) student at the University of the Witwatersrand, with over two years of programming experience in C#, C++, and Python, along with a foundation in data structures, algorithms, JavaScript and Assembly Language. From networked multiplayer games to AI-driven opponents, my academic and personal projects have continuously sharpened both my technical skills and creative thinking.

Outside of coding, I enjoy gaming, anime, staying active, being outdoors, and any opportunity to give back to the community. In 2024, I worked as a part-time tutor for BroadLearning, and between 2017 and 2020 I volunteered at Kid's Haven.From 2021-2024, I volunteered at the SOS Children's Village in Ennerdale. Take a look at my projects to see what I have been building, or feel free to reach out through my contact info!
